Frequently Asked Questions

Where are you located?

Our main store operates out of Topcity Plaza Basement Ed 52, Kampala, Uganda. Come visit us during our working hours to shop the collection in person!

How fast is delivery?

For orders placed within Kampala, we offer fast same-day delivery if ordered before 3:00 PM. Upcountry deliveries typically take between 2 to 3 working days.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused bags holding their original tags. Please reach out to us on WhatsApp, and we'll happily handle your exchange or return easily.

Do you restock sold out items?

We bring in fresh new arrivals weekly. While signature pieces often get restocked, some limited trend edits are exclusive and won't return once they are sold out!
